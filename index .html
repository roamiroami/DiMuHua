賞石之道

賞石協會 × ROAMI

花蓮 × 台灣

The Hualien Stone Appreciation Society (花蓮賞石協會) has long celebrated the quiet beauty of naturally formed stones collected from the rivers and mountains of Hualien, Taiwan. Each stone is a living record of geological time — formed from minerals compressed, polished, and carried by the Xiuguluan and Hualien rivers.

In collaboration with ROAMI 遊米, we present this digital museum as an accessible archive — inviting you to slow down, look closely, and discover the world held within each stone.

花蓮賞石協會長期致力於推廣台灣本土石藝文化，珍視每一顆來自花蓮山川河床的天然奇石。這些石頭是地質時間的見證者——由礦物質在秀姑巒溪與花蓮溪中歷經壓縮、研磨與搬運，最終形成獨一無二的自然藝術品。

ROAMI 遊米與花蓮賞石協會攜手合作，打造這座數位石藏館，邀請您放慢腳步，細細欣賞每一顆石頭所蘊藏的世界。
